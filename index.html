Nicole Fortin

Brooklyn, NY

Hello there! I was born and raised in a small town in Maine. Tired of opening my door to 5 feet of fresh snow every few days, I ventured to New York City and have been living in Brooklyn for 6 years now.

I'm pretty resourceful and have a knack for figuring things out and coming up with solutions, and this lead me to be involved with startup operations for companies including Upworthy, Fab, and TheLadders. For anyone who has ever worked in operations at a startup, you'll know just how exhausting the workload can be! I've finally decided to embrace my visually creative side and move towards a career path that engulfs both my naturally artistic and business-savvy instincts.

A few fun facts: I'm a strong advocate for year-round pumpkin products; I'm addicted to ice lattes; I'll be the first person to tell you about a new bar/restaurant/store opening in the area; and I was voted most likely to become a teacher -- perhaps the most boring and complimentary superlative ever created.
